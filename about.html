About Us

Crafted with ❤️ by Dev Bros

Welcome to BunkPlanner – your smart companion for managing attendance the right way. We built this tool to help students plan their bunks wisely, stay above attendance thresholds, and find the perfect balance between fun and responsibility.

As students ourselves, we know the struggle of keeping track of attendance and the constant worry of meeting the minimum requirement – or ending up paying a condonation fine for falling short. That’s why we designed BunkPlanner – a simple, accurate, and student-friendly solution.

Dev Bros is a small team of passionate developers who love building tools that make life easier. Our goal is to create projects that are both useful and enjoyable to use.

This is just the beginning – we’re constantly improving and adding features. Stay tuned for more updates, and thank you for supporting our journey! 🚀
